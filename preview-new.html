How It Works

Every gathering is
designed with intention.

From the guest list to the room format, every decision is made to ensure the gathering delivers real value.

01

Curated

Every seat matters. Guests are selected based on relevance, not reach. The room is built so every conversation has potential.

02

Structured

Each format has a flow — an opening, a theme, a facilitated moment — so participants leave with something real.

03

Facilitated

A host guides the room. That might be a question, a provocation, or a moment of shared insight that creates the conditions for connection.

Gathering Formats

Business,
done differently.

From curated dinners to interactive experiences, each event is designed to create opportunities while keeping it dynamic, social, and engaging.

Signature Experiences

Immersive evenings in exceptional venues.

Cultural venues, expert facilitation, and small-group leadership conversations. Limited to 30 guests.

Leadership Rooms

Intimate roundtables on a single theme.

10–15 leaders. Off the record. Highly curated. Built for depth over breadth.

Masterclasses

Expert-led sessions for practitioners.

A practitioner shares methodology, insight, or experience. Followed by structured Q&A.

Connection Gatherings

Curated social occasions for the community.

Designed to build relationships across LBL. Hosted in exceptional venues across Lisbon.
