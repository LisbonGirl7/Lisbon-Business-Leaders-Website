Who We Are

A Network Built on Substance, Not Just Status

We believe that true success is built on meaningful connections and partnerships among the city's most ambitious professionals. Lisbon Business Leaders is the city's premier membership organisation for senior executives, founders, and investors.

We are the first chapter of what will become Business Leaders International — a global network uniting the most influential business communities across the world's great cities.
